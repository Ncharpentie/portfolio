Backround

Chicken Satay is a type of food that can be food almost anywhere in southeast asia.

There are other types of satay, such as pork or beef.

Chicken satay can be found at any Thai restaurant in the United States.

Satay Chicken is originally from Indonesia.

There are many different versions of chicken satay.

This style of chicken satay commonly found in Thailand

Satay is commonly sold at stalls in markets and is refered to as street food

Be careful not to undercook the chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Red curry paste
  • Curry powder
  • Coconut milk
  • Salt and sugar

Making the Chicken

  1. Marinade the chicken in the ingrediants from anywhere between 20 minute to 3 hours. The longer in marinades the better
  2. Cook the chicken on the grill
