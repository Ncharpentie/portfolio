My Career

In order to become a doctor you need a bachelors degrees (any degree would work, but its better to have one relating to medicine/biology) You also will need to pass the medical collage admission exam. After passing the exam you will then spend spend 4 years in medical school and then spend at least 3 years working as a medical resident in a hospital under a licensed doctor. All this education is worth it though, as the average salary of a doctor is $181,201 which is a lot of money. Instead of doing the same thing every day like in a office job I could instead help people and do something more interesting then sit all day, while making a good amount of money. For this job you would need advanced medical knowledge as well as some knowledge on specific procedure/medical areas like surgery and eye care.

The average salary of a physician is $182,172. A physician is just general doctor, like the doctors you see during a check up. The average salary of a surgeon is $298,264. Surgeons are doctors and are trained to perform surgeries. All surgeons are docters, but not all doctors are surgeons. The average salary of a oncologist is $233,928. Oncologists are docters that are specifically trained to spot and treat cancer. The average salary of a neurologist is $253,994. Neurologists are doctors that have extensive knowledge about the nervous system The average salary of an a aesthesiologist is $367,711.
